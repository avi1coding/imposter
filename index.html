IMPOSTER

Everyone gets the same word, except the imposter. Find out who they are.

Select a mode

3–99 players · one device

How to play

Everyone shares one phone and passes it around.

  • Everyone gets a secret word

    A random category and word are chosen. Most players see the word.

  • …except the imposter(s)

    The imposter doesn't get the word — just that they're the imposter (and maybe a small hint).

  • Pass & peek

    Tap your card, tap the box to secretly reveal your role, then pass the phone on.

  • Take turns describing

    Going clockwise, each player says one word related to the secret word — vague enough to be safe, clear enough to prove you know it.

  • Imposter blends in

    The imposter fakes it, trying to guess the word from what others say without getting caught.

  • Discuss & vote

    After a couple of rounds, talk it out and vote on who the imposter is.

  • Reveal

    Players win if they catch the imposter. The imposter wins by surviving — or by guessing the word.

  • Everyone gets a question

    A random category and question are chosen. Most players get the same question.

  • …except the imposter(s)

    The imposter secretly gets a different question in the same category — with a similar-sounding answer. They don't even know they're the imposter!

  • Pass, reveal & answer

    Tap your card, reveal your question, type a short answer, then pass the phone on.

  • Answers revealed

    Once everyone's answered, the real question and all the answers are shown together.

  • Spot the odd one out

    The imposter answered a different question — so their answer won't quite fit. Discuss and vote.

  • Reveal

    See who the imposter was and the sneaky question they actually had.

  • Everyone gets a word

    A random category and word are chosen — most players get the same word.

  • …except the imposter(s)

    The imposter secretly gets a closely related word (Dog vs Wolf, Coffee vs Tea). They don't know it's different!

  • Pass & peek

    Tap your card, reveal your word, then pass the phone on.

  • Describe your word

    Going clockwise, each player says one word about theirs — the imposter's clues will drift off.

  • Discuss & vote

    Whose clues don't match everyone else's word?

  • Reveal

    See who the imposter was and the sneaky look-alike word they had.

  • Everyone gets a scale

    Two opposite ends (like Cheap ↔ Expensive) and one thing to rate on it.

  • …except the imposter(s)

    The imposter secretly rates a different thing on the same scale — and doesn't know it.

  • Slide & pass

    Reveal your item, drag the slider to where it lands, then pass the phone.

  • All dials revealed

    Every player's dot appears on the scale at once.

  • Spot the outlier

    The imposter rated something else, so their dot sits in the wrong spot. Discuss and vote.

  • Reveal

    See who the imposter was and what they were actually rating.
